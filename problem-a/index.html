Snoopy

Snoopy is a character from the comic strip Peanuts, by Charles Schulz. Snoopy is a dog of the Beagle breed and belongs to the main character of the comic strip, Charlie Brown. Snoopy is known for being a sassy and humorous friend within the Peanuts bunch.
